VR/AR-Browser is a web browser that transforms websites or search terms into VR-ready 3D environments. Users who land on the same website will be able to interact and talk with one another. This experiment is inspired by World Wide Maze and the ever popular VR Chat.

In addition to its VR components, AR mode gives a real-time view of all users on a particular website or search term. Unfortunately, the platform doesn't support markerless AR at the moment. To use AR with this project, please print out or save a Hiro Marker and point your camera at it.
