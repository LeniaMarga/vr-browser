VR/AR-Browser is an interactive multi-user web experience that transforms websites into navigable 3D environments. The size of each environment depends on the user's browser size and the length of each webpage. Users that load up the same webpage are able to see and voice chat one another. This experiment is heavily inspired by World Wide Maze and the ever popular VR Chat.

In addition to its VR components, AR mode gives the user a top-down admin-like view of all the players on a particular website. Unfortunately, the platform doesn't support markerless AR at the moment. To use AR with this project, please print out a Hiro Marker and point your camera at it.
